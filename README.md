methods, system-level designs, and technical notes aligned with academic research
and standards-driven professional practice.

This repository emphasizes **clarity, rigor, and long-term value**, bridging
the gap between theory, simulation, and real-world engineering systems.

---

## Scope and Focus

This wiki covers topics across multiple engineering domains, with particular
emphasis on:

- **RF, Microwave, and Antenna Engineering**
- **MIMO, Phased Arrays, and mmWave Systems**
- **Communication Networks (LTE / 5G / 6G, SDN, QoS)**
- **Signal Processing and Modulation Techniques**
- **Systems Engineering and Architecture**
- **AI-Enabled and Intelligent Engineering Systems**
- **Ethical AI Integration and Responsible Automation**
- **Research Methodology, Simulation, and Documentation**

The content is written to support:
- Researchers and PhD students
- Practicing engineers
- Standards-aligned technical work
- Long-form documentation and reproducibility
